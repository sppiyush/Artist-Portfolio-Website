Hey! my name is Bharati Arya from Jaipur,India. I have been blending traditional techniques with contemporary expression. My work reflects a deep reverence for culture and heritage, often drawing inspiration from mythology and folklore.

My sculptures are actually a symphony of form and emotion, capturing the essence of subjects with exquisite detail and skill. You can observe a profound connection between art and the human experience in my creations. Get in touch today with the kind of sculptures and paintings you are looking for your home or office.
